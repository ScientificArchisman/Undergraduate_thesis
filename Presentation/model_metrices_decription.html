Performance Metrics

Accuracy

Accuracy is the proportion of correctly classified instances out of all instances.

$$\frac{TP + TN}{TP + TN + FP + FN}$$

Precision

Precision is the proportion of true positives out of all positive predictions.

$$\frac{TP}{TP + FP}$$

Recall

Recall is the proportion of true positives out of all actual positives.

$$\frac{TP}{TP + FN}$$

F1 Score

The F1 score is the harmonic mean of precision and recall, and is a useful metric when there is an uneven class distribution (i.e. when some classes have many more instances than others).

$$2 \times \frac{\text{precision} \times \text{recall}}{\text{precision} + \text{recall}}$$
