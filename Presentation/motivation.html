Increased Cardiovascular Diseases

Cardiovascular disease statistics: Cardiovascular disease is the leading cause of death worldwide, accounting for an estimated 17.9 million deaths each year. (World Health Organization) In the United States, cardiovascular disease is responsible for approximately 1 in 3 deaths, or about 655,000 deaths per year. (Centers for Disease Control and Prevention) Coronary heart disease is the most common type of cardiovascular disease, accounting for approximately 45% of all cardiovascular deaths in the United States. (American Heart Association)
